We use eco-friendly materials.

We use eco-friendly gypsum board obtained HB mark (the highest grade) and eco-friendly PVC film obtained the eco-friendly mark in accordance with the environmental mark and eco-friendly building material certification system.

We are proud of the best value for money.

It is easy to construct by using an all-in-one product combining gypsum board and PVC film, and economical as the construction period can be shortened.

We constantly research innovative technologies.

The technologies such as insulation, waterproofing, sound blocking, antibacterial and fireproofing have been combined, and we continue to research more advanced technologies.

It is safe for fire.

Thanks to early fire prevention as an advantage of existing gypsum board containing about 20% crystallized water, combustion delay performance, and non-flammability as an advantage of semi-non-combustible PVC film, it has excellent fire-resistance and fire prevention performances.

We provide better technology and easier construction method.

AHA board can be cut easily with a knife, is easy to install on the wall or ceiling, and the finishing can be given as soon as the construction is over, so it needs no construction using finishing materials such as paint or wallpaper.
